Introduction

Welcome! My name is Alice and I am a part-time freelance digital artist. I have been taking commissions for over four years and I specialize in drawing animals and fantasy artworks. Using the Internet as a tool for learning different styles and freelance tips from other artists is something that I will always be grateful for.
