Introduction

Welcome! My name is Alice and I have been drawing and offering commissions online for several years. When I was first starting out I scoured the internet for tutorials from artists that I looked up to. Those tutorials helped me improve and shaped my habits and style. After many years of practice, I feel as though I am at a the skill level where I feel comfortable sharing some tips and advice of my own, be that you are a beginner, intermediate, or perhaps even an advanced artist.
